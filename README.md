I have analyzed global EV charging data to understand: Charging cost variations  Charger utilization , Temperature impacts on session reliability

##  Key Findings
- Charging costs vary significantly across countries due to differences in energy pricing and infrastructure maturity.
- DC fast chargers deliver higher energy per session compared to AC chargers.
- Charging session failures increase at temperature extremes, highlighting the need for climate-resilient charging infrastructure.
- Certain EV manufacturers show consistently shorter charging durations, likely due to higher charging power compatibility.
